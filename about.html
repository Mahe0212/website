ABOUT INIKA

MISSION

The main mission of INIKA EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTION is to serve our country and society through the pursuit of education, learning and research at the highest level of excellence. And maintain a high class Department with excellence in teaching and research part of our university.

VISION

Our INSTITUTION vision is to enhance our reputation world-wide as best teaching and research institution for our innovations, discovery. And offer every student in our institution the best teaching by our staff and make every student innovative and creative.
